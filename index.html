Frequently Asked Questions

What is DataFlow and how does it work? ▼
DataFlow is an AI-powered analytics platform that transforms raw data into actionable insights. Our intelligent algorithms analyze patterns in your data and present them in easy-to-understand visualizations, enabling faster decision-making.
How long does it take to see results? ▼
Most users see meaningful insights within the first 24 hours of connecting their data sources. Our platform is designed for quick deployment and immediate value generation, reducing what traditionally took months into days or hours.
Is my data secure with DataFlow? ▼
Security is our top priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption for data in transit and at rest, comply with GDPR and SOC 2 standards, and never share your data with third parties. Your information remains under your complete control.
What kind of data sources does DataFlow support? ▼
DataFlow integrates with virtually any data source including databases (SQL, NoSQL), cloud services (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud), CRM platforms, and custom APIs. We continuously add new integrations based on user demand.
Can I try DataFlow for free? ▼
Yes! Our free trial gives you full access to all features for 14 days with no credit card required. You can upload sample data and explore the platform's capabilities without any commitment.
