Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DataFlow and how we can help transform your business.

What is DataFlow?
DataFlow is an AI-powered analytics platform that transforms raw data into actionable insights. Our platform helps businesses make smarter decisions by providing real-time data analysis, visualization, and predictive insights across all your business operations.
Who should use DataFlow?
DataFlow is designed for businesses of all sizes - from startups to enterprises. Whether you're a marketing professional analyzing campaign performance, a sales manager tracking KPIs, or a data scientist building complex models, DataFlow provides tools tailored to your needs.
How long does it take to get started?
You can get started with DataFlow in minutes. Simply sign up for a free account, connect your data sources, and begin analyzing within 15 minutes. Our onboarding wizard guides you through each step and provides templates for common use cases.
What are your pricing plans?
We offer four pricing plans: Free (limited features), Starter ($29/month for small teams), Professional ($99/month for growing businesses), and Enterprise (custom pricing). All paid plans include priority support, advanced features, and dedicated account management. You can view detailed pricing at our pricing page.
Is there a free trial available?
Yes! All new users get a 14-day free trial of our Professional plan with full access to all features. No credit card required. After the trial ends, you can choose to upgrade to a paid plan or continue with our free plan.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate your billing accordingly. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.
What data sources does DataFlow support?
DataFlow integrates with 500+ data sources including databases (SQL Server, PostgreSQL, MySQL), cloud platforms (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure), business tools (Salesforce, HubSpot, Stripe), and file formats (CSV, JSON, Excel). We add new integrations regularly based on user demand.
Is my data secure in DataFlow?
Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES 256-bit encryption). We're compliant with SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, HIPAA, and CCPA standards. We perform regular security audits and penetration testing to ensure your data remains protected.
Can I use DataFlow's API?
Yes! DataFlow offers a comprehensive REST API for Professional and Enterprise plans. You can programmatically create reports, query data, manage users, and build custom integrations. Full API documentation and code examples are available in our developer portal.
What support options are available?
We offer multiple support channels: email support (all plans), live chat (Starter and above), and phone support (Professional and Enterprise). Response times vary by plan, with Enterprise customers receiving 24/7 dedicated support with 1-hour response time guaranteed.
Is there training available?
We provide comprehensive training resources including video tutorials, documentation, webinars, and certification programs. Enterprise customers also receive personalized training sessions with our specialists. All resources are available in our learning center.
What is your uptime guarantee?
DataFlow guarantees 99.9% uptime for all paid plans. Our infrastructure is hosted on leading cloud providers with automatic failover and redundancy. In the unlikely event of downtime, Enterprise customers receive service credits as outlined in our SLA.
