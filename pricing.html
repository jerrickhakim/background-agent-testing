Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime? ▼
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, we'll prorate the additional charges.
Is there a free trial available? ▼
Absolutely! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You'll have full access to all features in your selected plan during the trial period.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual subscriptions. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
Do you offer discounts for annual billing? ▼
Yes! Customers who choose annual billing save 20% compared to monthly billing. You can toggle between monthly and yearly pricing at the top of this page.
What happens if I exceed my project limit? ▼
We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can easily upgrade to a higher plan to get more projects. Alternatively, you can contact our sales team about a custom plan.
Is there a refund policy? ▼
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on annual plans. If you're not satisfied, we'll provide a full refund. Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled anytime with no penalty.
